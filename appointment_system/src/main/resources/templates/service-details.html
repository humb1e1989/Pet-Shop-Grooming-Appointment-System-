Dog Boarding

Dog boarding refers to the practice of leaving your dog in the care of a professional boarding facility or individual while you are away from home. This can be for a few days or weeks, depending on the length of your trip. Dog boarding facilities typically provide food, shelter, exercise, and attention to your pet while you are away. Some facilities may also offer additional services such as grooming or training.
